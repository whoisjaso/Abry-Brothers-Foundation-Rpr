# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Pasadena and Houston-area homeowners, property owners, and managers who are worried about foundation movement, cracks, drainage problems, sloping floors, or house leveling needs. They are usually deciding whether to call a contractor now, request an inspection, or compare repair options before damage gets worse.

## Product Purpose

Abry Brothers Foundation Rpr is a single-page lead-generation website for a foundation repair contractor in Pasadena, Texas. The site must make foundation repair, structural repair, drainage, and home leveling services immediately clear, then drive visitors to call or request a free foundation inspection.

Success means a visitor can quickly understand the company, trust the local service area and reviews, see relevant repair services, and take one practical next step.

## Brand Personality

Structural, honest, local, durable, and contractor-grade. The voice should feel direct and practical, with enough warmth to reassure homeowners but no playful or luxury tone.

## Anti-references

This should not look like a water fountain, landscaping, pool, or decorative water-feature website. It should not look like a generic contractor template, a soft luxury home-services page, or a bright playful consumer brand. Avoid fake photos, invented reviews, invented hours, overstuffed hero badges, and service-board images that shrink into unreadable mobile graphics.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next action obvious: call or request a free inspection.
2. Preserve the structural brand: navy blueprint, copper accents, concrete textures, foundation piers, and bold contractor typography.
3. Keep claims practical and verifiable: use supplied reviews, supplied location details, supplied pricing ranges, and clear inspection disclaimers.
4. Treat mobile as the primary conversion path: sticky actions, large tap targets, short form flow, and collapsible service details.
5. Build trust through clarity: explain warning signs, process, service areas, and repair categories without burying the phone number.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, semantic form labels, and responsive text that does not overflow on mobile.
